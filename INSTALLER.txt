
	
To install the app for Windows you will have to download the installer and execute 
it. The link to the installer is the following :

https://drive.google.com/file/d/10XhNjGjl1bVU93sxXVVfErg-_rGsHupQ/view?usp=sharing

Once you have installed the app move to the installation path which you previously
selected. You can execute the chatclient.exe directly or create a shortcut in your
Desktop.
